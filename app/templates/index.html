Find Your Bob

The discography of our greatest living songwriter can be large and overwhelming, where is a newcomer to dive into the great river of Bob? Maybe you know of Bob’s work from the ‘60s but don’t know where to dive into the other stuff. Here at Find Your Bob, we offer some assistance, log in with your Spotify account and we’ll show you the album that best matches your Spotify listening history, as well as make you a personalized Bob playlist.
